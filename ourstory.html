Our Story

Package Courier provides expedited Air Cargo services for Customers who need fast, friendly and reliable service.

We are unique to the Air Cargo industry thanks to our People who consistently provide exceptional Customer Service and our flight schedule which is unmatched allowing for expedited delivery to destination.

Our Vision

To become the world's most loved, most relied upon airline, providing specialized air cargo products with friendly, efficient, and relentlessly reliable airport-to-airport service.

Our Mission

The mission of Package Courier is dedication to the highest level of Customer service delivered with a sense of warmth, friendliness, individual pride, and Company Spirit.
